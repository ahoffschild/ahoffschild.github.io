Broken Iron

A demo for a future project, created in Unity. A 2D action game with fast platforming and faster combat, taking movement and stage cues from games like megaman while incorporating fighting game inspired attack systems allowing you weave together flashy combos.

Demo has 1 test stage and a boss alongside a basic moveset for the protagonist - I am looking for release a newer demo in the future with more original assets, a complete moveset, and a more polished stage.

Sidesplitters

A game where a struggling comedian's night gets worse as they receive an unexpected visitor. Quickly think of a solid punchline to make the monster laugh - it's a tough crowd.

Created in 48 hours for the Global Game Jam Edmonton 2024. The game was created in Unity, and involved a team of five with me serving as the only programmer. Visual and sound assets were provided by the remainder of the team.

Delivery Gone Afowl

A hard-working delivery owl finds itself in a tough spot as it's deliveries are interrupted by a monster searching through the forest. Take flight to make your deliveries in time, but be careful - the forest's guardian is always getting more impatient.

Developed over the course of 2 weeks for the Alberta Slow Jam 2024 with a team of 2. All programming done by me as my first Godot game, and all visual assets were either me or provided by teammate kelsiegosse.

Elite Zebra Referee Project

A capstone project developed during my time at the Northern Alberta Institute of Technology. The game itself will not be directly linked (or thoroughly described) at the request of the client, but details may be shown off as part of my portfolio upon request.

I was the primary programmer for the core systems of the project: a gameplay loop that can assist in training referees, alongside additional features for review and practice such as highly customizable options and a replay saving and playback system.
